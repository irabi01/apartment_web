Details about the property

The apartment are accessible through Konge East road and offer a convenience, peace of mind and excellent view of the landscape and Lake Victoria on the other site. The apartments are for a long and short stay.

The apartments have the following:

  • 3 beds with high quality linens, blankets, pillows and hangers in closet;
  • 2 bathrooms with showers, cotton towels;
  • Living room with TV connected to DStv cable,high quality sofas, dining, coffee side tables;
  • Kitchen: It has refrigerator/freezer, stove/oven, microwave, kettle, cookwares and cutlery;
  • In additional, there is iron and ironing board, WiFi, geyser and cleaning. Laundry service will be available at a fee.
